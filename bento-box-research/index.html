How It Works

A unique, scalable research system

No scoping, no weeks of negotiation. The framework is fixed — the same four dimensions for every product area — so setup takes days, not months. The first interview produces data; by interview twenty, the health signal is live and keeps sharpening.

Step 01
Intercept
A lightweight intercept is embedded on the client’s product. Targeting is configured for the right users at the right moments. The client approves the setup; we handle everything else.
Step 02
Interview
An AI moderator conducts a 10–15 minute interview, probing all four product health dimensions.
Step 03
Score
Each interview is scored 1–5 across four dimensions using behavioral anchors grounded in validated psychometric research. Every interview sharpens the next — frustration patterns cluster automatically.
Step 04
Act
The signal appears immediately on the dashboard. Clients see where users struggle and the qualitative depth to understand why — so product teams can fix it in the next sprint. At mid-market, a research partner layers on top to spot deeper patterns.
