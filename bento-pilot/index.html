How it works

Four steps. One install.

01
Install in one click
Install from the Shopify App Store, turn on Bento in your theme editor, and it just works. No custom development required.
02
Bento talks to real shoppers
A small subset of shoppers on your site get invited into a 5-minute conversation in exchange for a gift card you fund (default $15 of store credit, configurable). An AI moderator, trained in customer research, asks questions to understand their shopping behavior.
03
The expensive issues surface
Recurring frictions cluster into issues, ranked by what each one is costing you. Every interview also adds to a knowledge base of your shoppers, so the picture of your store gets sharper with every conversation.
04
Brainstorm a fix
Bento helps you brainstorm the best solution to each issue, giving you step-by-step instructions on what to do.
Quality control

Built so every conversation stays on track.

Bento is built so every interview produces useful signal, not noise.

Focus areas
Pick what to learn about: Ease, Trust, or Value.
Each interview is targeted to one of three focus areas. Ease (can shoppers do what they came to do?), Trust (do they believe you?), or Value (is it worth their time and money?). You set the focus and the moderator drives toward it.
Stay on track
The moderator keeps shoppers on topic.
When a shopper drifts off-topic, the AI moderator redirects them back toward the focus area with real follow-up questions. Not a script, not a survey.
Bad-faith filter
Gamers don't get paid, and don't pollute your data.
Keyboard mashing, refusals to engage, and prompt-injection attempts get one polite warning, then a hard-stop. No gift card, no record in your data, and a permanent ban from your widget.
Sincere shoppers paid
Real effort always earns the reward.
Sincere shoppers earn the gift card even when their answers are short or shaky. Every interview is graded after the fact for substance, so what shows up on your dashboard is the signal, not the noise.

How is this different?

You probably already have tools that try.

They don't replace each other. They're solving different jobs. Bento fills the one none of them can.

Heatmaps & replay
Hotjar, Lucky Orange, Microsoft Clarity
Show you where shoppers click and how far they scroll. They can't tell you what shoppers were thinking when they bounced.
Surveys & NPS
Zigpoll, Fairing, KnoCommerce
Surface-level signal. Multiple-choice answers and one-line feedback rarely tell you enough to actually fix the problem. You know something is wrong, but not why or what to do about it.
Reviews
Yotpo, Judge.me, Loox
Capture happy customers. The shoppers who didn't buy don't leave reviews, and "5 stars, love it" doesn't tell you what almost stopped the next one.
Bento
In-depth conversations with shoppers, mid-shop.
Real, open-ended interviews about why they're buying, or not. Scored, clustered, and turned into the fixes that move revenue.
Privacy & Security

Built for the trust your shoppers and your data require.

Protecting your shoppers' privacy and your data is foundational to how Bento is built.

Consent
Shoppers opt in plainly.
An 18+ consent screen runs before any conversation starts. The AI is identified up-front, and no human eavesdrops. Shoppers explicitly consent to having the conversation transcribed, or can type instead and skip the mic. Shopper email is never collected. The gift card code is shown on screen at the end.
Redaction
PII scrubbed before you ever see it.
Shoppers aren't asked for names, emails, phones, or payment details, and have no reason to share them. If something slips into a transcript anyway, a two-stage redactor (regex plus LLM) strips it before storage. A database write-guard rejects anything that fails.
Audio stays local
Speech is transcribed in the browser. The audio never reaches us.
When a shopper speaks, transcription happens right in their own browser. The audio itself never leaves their device, so there's nothing for us to store, leak, or analyze. Only the resulting text reaches our servers, encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM. Raw transcripts are deleted within 24 hours; redacted transcripts are kept on a 12-month rolling window.
Your data is yours
No transcripts cross merchant lines.
Your transcripts, quotes, and insights stay in your tenant. Per-shop data never pools with another merchant's. GDPR and CCPA compliant, with full deletion via Shopify webhook or one click in your admin.

Detailed Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, and Data Processing Agreement are drafted and available on request. Final counsel review pending before public launch. Ask anytime.

Frequently asked questions.

Will my shoppers actually do this?

Based on intercept patterns with a visible incentive, we anticipate roughly a 10% acceptance rate, meaning ~1 in 10 shoppers who see the invitation will start a conversation. Bento intercepts a small fraction of your traffic per week (you set the volume), so it's never blanketing every visitor.

The conversations are short, friendly, and reward shoppers for their time with a gift card to your store. Real-world acceptance numbers are part of what the pilot validates.

Won't this annoy my customers?

The intercept is small, in your brand colors, and easy to dismiss. To collect ~5 completed interviews per week (the default), Bento shows the intercept to roughly 50 shoppers per week. About 1 in 10 shoppers who see it will accept and start a conversation. Once a shopper has seen the invitation, they won't see it again.

How is this different from the survey tool I already have?

Surveys ask shoppers to pick from answers you wrote. The actual reason for a problem rarely fits one of those buckets. Bento has open-ended conversations. The AI moderator chases the real "why" with follow-up questions, the same way a professional customer researcher would.

What about privacy and PII?

See the Privacy & Security section above for the full picture. Short version: shoppers consent before they say a word. Speech is transcribed in their own browser, so the audio never leaves their device. Only the text transcript reaches our servers, and it's PII-redacted before you ever see it. Your data never pools with other merchants, and we're GDPR and CCPA compliant. Full Privacy Policy, Terms, and DPA available on request.

How much time does this take from me?

Setup is ~30 minutes the first day. After that, the only thing you have to do is read the insights when they land. Most of the value comes from a once-a-week check-in.

The dashboard is built to be scannable in 60 seconds. We'll also email you when something urgent surfaces.

What's the financial impact of the gift cards?

The default is a $15 store-credit gift card per completed interview. You set this in your settings; it can be lower or higher.

Two things to keep in mind. First, the codes are store credit, redeemable on your site, so most of that comes back as revenue when shoppers use them. Second, at the default cap of ~5 interviews/week that's about $300/mo of credit issued. Even one shipped fix per quarter typically clears that several times over.

What if I don't get useful insights?

Patterns get reliable somewhere around the second or third interview that surfaces the same theme. If after the pilot you haven't seen a fix worth shipping, we'll work with you to figure out what to adjust: targeting, question sequence, sample size.

Shopify is the primary integration, with a one-click install via the Shopify App Store. WooCommerce, Wix, and BigCommerce are on the roadmap.

If you're on a non-Shopify platform and want to be in the pilot, mention it in your application and we'll talk through whether it makes sense.
