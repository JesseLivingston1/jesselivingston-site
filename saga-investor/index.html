The Solution

The first UX metric with qualitative depth

Saga deploys AI agents onto any product area. They run continuous intercept interviews with real users while the experience is fresh, and score every interview across four dimensions grounded in validated psychometric research.

How It Works

From silent metrics to continuous understanding

Step 01
Intercept
A lightweight widget intercepts users on the product itself, seconds after they experience a flow. Fresh recall, no surveys, no recruitment delays.
Step 02
Interview
An AI moderator conducts a 10–15 minute qualitative interview. Probes for the why behind behavior. Follows the participant, not a script.
Step 03
Score
Each interview is scored 1–5 across four dimensions using behavioral anchors grounded in validated psychometric research.
Step 04
Act
A living health score appears on your dashboard with the qualitative depth to understand why — enabling you to act before problems compound.
